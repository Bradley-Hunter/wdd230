Whitespace

BYU-Idaho

byui.edu

BYUI use of whitespace allows for users to know what belongs to each topic or area. The proper use of whitespace leaves plenty of room for a user to clink on what they want to click on.

Fitt's Law

DnD Beyond

dndbeyond.com

DnD Beyond follows Fitt's Law in sizing things for a user to click on what they want to click on while also not making it so that things are either too big or too small.

Contrast

Rust

rust-lang.org

Rust does a great job at making the colors used for the background and text contrast with each other in a way that is both pleasing to the eye along with being easy to read.
